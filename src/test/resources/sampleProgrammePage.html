    Classic Album: Steel Pulse - Handsworth Revolution

    Steve Lamacq

    Steve replays his Classic Album of the Day interview with Steel Pulse, about their debut 1978 album Handsworth Revolution.

    Steve replays his Classic Album of the Day interview with Steel Pulse, about their debut 1978 album Handsworth Revolution.

    Handsworth Revolution is an album by Steel Pulse, named after the Handsworth district of Birmingham, England, the band's home who the album was dedicated to. It is classed as the seminal British reggae record.
